## About The Strength Period
The Strength Period is a client-side strength training application that generates periodized
training plans for runners and endurance athletes. All data lives locally in the browser.
Presets are reusable training plan templates that the app uses to scaffold sessions for a user
over a defined number of weeks, combining exercises into a coherent, progressive program.

## What a preset represents
A preset defines the "shape" of a training block: which muscle groups to prioritize, which
exercise pool to draw from, how long the block runs, and what progression strategy to apply.
The app uses presets to auto-generate weekly sessions adapted to the user's profile and
restrictions. One preset may produce very different sessions for a healthy runner vs. one
recovering from a knee injury, depending on autoRestrictions filtering.

## Target user profile
- Recreational and trail runners (tags: corredor, pujada, baixada are highly relevant)
- Athletes complementing running with gym-free strength work
- Users with common overuse injuries: knee tendinopathies, ankle issues, low back
- Beginner to intermediate training age
